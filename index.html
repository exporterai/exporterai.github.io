About us
Exporter.AI is an Indian startup that leverages technology to simplify the information flow of cross-border supply chains (EXIM trade). Starting with Indian exporters and gradually expanding to serve global exporters, Exporter.AI ensures seamless and efficient operations while staying attuned to the evolving needs of the global EXIM trade landscape.

While simplifying information flow of Exim trade for exporters we know that there is no shortage of data availability for exporters about their business. In fact, data is available more than required but it is not structured or formulated as per their business requirements. This is where Exporter.AI comes in the picture. We are a bunch of technology-oriented people who are ready to support exporters to know, understand and grow your business with our business intelligence tool. Exporter.AI will support you to know your competition and access your competitor mapping by keeping your business volume as reference.

Exporter.AI will be adding features likes importer discovery, importers mapping and sales funnels based on these. As your business grows we will be happy to support you with compliance and financial solutions, all of this leveraging technology.
